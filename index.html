Delete Your Tikto King Account

To delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps directly inside the app:

  1. On the main menu, press your player badge with your name and profile picture in the top left corner.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the profile screen and press the "Delete account" button.

Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove your username, gameplay progress, and all linked data from our servers.
